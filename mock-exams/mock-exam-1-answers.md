# MS-102 Mock Exam – Test 1  
## Answers & Explanations

---

## Question 1
**Correct Answer:** B – Conditional Access policy  

**Explanation:**  
Conditional Access allows administrators to block legacy authentication
methods that do not support modern security controls like MFA.

---

## Question 2
**Correct Answer:** C – Privileged Identity Management (PIM)  

**Explanation:**  
PIM provides just-in-time access, reducing permanent admin privilege exposure
and improving security.

---

## Question 3
**Correct Answer:** B – Identity Protection  

**Explanation:**  
Identity Protection detects risky sign-ins and can automatically remediate
compromised accounts.
